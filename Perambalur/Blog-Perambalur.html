What Is the Role of a Healthcare Technician?

Healthcare technicians trained at Profenaa Nursing and Health Science College in Perambalur are equipped to deliver high quality technical support across multiple healthcare settings. They assist doctors and clinical staff in diagnostics, surgeries, emergency care, and rehabilitation. They operate, maintain, and calibrate advanced medical equipment and ensure a safe, sterile, and efficient environment for all patients. They also maintain accurate clinical records and contribute to the overall quality of patient care.
